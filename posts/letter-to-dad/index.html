letter to dad

January 1, 0001

300 words

2 minutes to read

Hi Dady,

I am the richest daughter in the world. I am not rich because I have lots of money but am richest because I have the BEST dad in the world. Whatever am today is because of you and mom. Your unconditional love and style is something I admire Dady. There is none like you. While writing this, I recollect a photo in which your holding me in your Kawasaki bike. From then till now, everything you taught me is right there in my heart. I owe my success and happiness to you Dady. The way you treat mom is a great example of how a husband and wife should be. KARATE, this art that you have taught me has done miracles Dady. You have taught me ethics and values of life in the form of karate. For me your the best teacher as well Dady. I know how proud you felt when Shiroma master said my “cat stance is perfect “. I didn’t really know how important it was to get the compliment from shiroma master but when you told people about it and felt proud of me, that’s the moment that bought pride that I did something good. Dady your fearless , your Charismatic attitude , the way you lead your life with content are the motivation for me to get moving come what may.

Yours loving daughter, Preethi Raja
